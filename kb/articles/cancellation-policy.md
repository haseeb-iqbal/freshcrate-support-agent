# Cancellation Policy

## How to cancel
You can cancel your subscription any time from **Account → Subscription → Cancel**. Cancellation is immediate for future boxes — there are no cancellation fees and no minimum commitment. If you only want to skip a few weeks, pausing is usually a better fit than cancelling.

## What happens to your current box
Cancelling stops all future boxes, but any box that has already passed the Wednesday 11:59pm weekly cutoff will still be delivered and charged, because it's already been packed. Boxes that haven't locked yet are cancelled and not charged.

## Reactivating after cancelling
Cancelled accounts can be reactivated at any time from **Account → Subscription → Reactivate**. Your saved preferences and delivery address are kept for 12 months after cancellation. After 12 months you may need to set up your plan again.

## Cancel vs. pause
If you're going away or just want a break, pause instead of cancelling — pausing keeps your plan and preferences and resumes automatically. Cancel if you don't plan to return soon.
