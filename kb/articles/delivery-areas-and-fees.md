# Delivery Areas & Shipping Fees

## Where FreshCrate delivers
FreshCrate delivers to most addresses in the contiguous United States. You can check whether your ZIP code is covered on the sign-up page or under **Account → Delivery Settings**. We don't currently deliver to Alaska, Hawaii, or PO boxes.

## Shipping fees
Shipping is a flat fee per box that's shown at checkout and on your weekly invoice; it doesn't change with plan size. Orders above a weekly threshold ship free — the current free-shipping threshold is shown on the Plans page.

## Delivery windows
Boxes arrive between 8am and 8pm on your delivery day. We don't offer guaranteed narrow time windows, but your tracking email includes an estimated arrival range that narrows on the day of delivery.

## Address changes and coverage
If you move, update your address under **Account → Delivery Settings** before the Wednesday cutoff. If your new address is outside our delivery area, we'll let you know and can pause your subscription until coverage is available.
