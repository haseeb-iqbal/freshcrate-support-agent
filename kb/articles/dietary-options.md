# Dietary Options & Recipe Preferences

## Available preferences
FreshCrate offers vegetarian, pescatarian, low-carb, calorie-smart, and family-friendly recipe preferences. Setting a preference filters the weekly menu so the recipes you're offered match your diet. You can set or change preferences from **Account → Preferences**.

## Allergens and ingredient information
Every recipe lists its ingredients and major allergens (such as nuts, dairy, gluten, soy, and shellfish) on the recipe card and in the app. FreshCrate prepares ingredients in facilities that handle common allergens, so we can't guarantee a recipe is fully free of cross-contact.

## Choosing your weekly recipes
Even with a preference set, you can hand-pick each week's recipes from the menu before the Wednesday cutoff. If you don't choose, we auto-select recipes that match your preference. There's no extra charge for vegetarian or low-carb recipes.

## Customizing or swapping proteins
Some recipes let you swap the default protein (for example, chicken for tofu) for a small surcharge shown at checkout. Protein swaps are set per recipe, per week, and don't change your overall plan.
