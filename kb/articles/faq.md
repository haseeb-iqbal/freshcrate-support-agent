# Frequently Asked Questions

## Do I have to commit to a contract?
No. FreshCrate is fully flexible — there's no contract or minimum number of boxes. You can pause or cancel any time, and changes follow the weekly Wednesday 11:59pm cutoff.

## Can I skip a single week?
Yes. Skipping one week is the same as a one-week pause. Skip before the Wednesday cutoff from **Account → Subscription** and you won't be charged for that week. Your subscription resumes automatically the following week.

## What if I'm not home for delivery?
You don't need to be home. Boxes are insulated to stay cold for several hours. You can add delivery notes (for example, "leave at side door") under **Account → Delivery Settings**.

## Can I have more than one box on different days?
Each subscription has a single plan and one delivery day. If you want boxes on different days, you'd need separate subscriptions. Most customers instead adjust their plan size to get more meals in one weekly box.

## How do I contact a human?
You can reach a FreshCrate support specialist any time by asking to talk to a human in chat, or from **Account → Help → Contact Support**. Sensitive account changes and large refunds are always handled by a person.
