# Getting Started with FreshCrate

## How FreshCrate works
FreshCrate is a weekly meal-kit subscription. Each week you get a box with pre-portioned ingredients and step-by-step recipe cards for the meals you chose. You pick your plan size, set your preferences, and a box ships on your delivery day every week until you pause or cancel.

## Setting up your first box
After signing up, choose a plan (meals per week and servings), choose your dietary track (Standard, Gluten-Free, Vegetarian or Dairy-Free), and pick your delivery day. You can hand-select your recipes or let FreshCrate auto-pick based on your preferences. Your first box ships on the next available delivery day after the weekly cutoff.

## What's in a box
Each box contains chilled, pre-portioned ingredients packed with insulation and ice packs to stay fresh in transit, plus a recipe card for each meal. Most recipes take 25–40 minutes to cook. Ingredients stay fresh in the fridge for several days after delivery.

## Managing everything from your account
Your **Account** area is where you change your plan, preferences, delivery day, and address, pause or cancel, and view orders and billing. Most common requests — pausing, switching plans, reporting a problem — can also be handled by asking support directly.
