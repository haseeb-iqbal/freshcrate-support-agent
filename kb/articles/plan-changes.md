# Changing Your Plan

## Plan sizes
FreshCrate offers 2, 3, and 4 meals per week, each available for 2 or 4 servings per meal. Your weekly price depends on the number of meals and servings. You can see current pricing for each plan on the **Plans** page before you switch.

## Switching meals per week or servings
Change your plan any time from **Account → Plan**. Switching the number of meals or servings takes effect from your next unlocked box — the Wednesday 11:59pm weekly cutoff applies, so changes made after the cutoff start the following week.

## How billing changes when you switch
When you change plans, the new price applies to the first box that ships on the new plan. You're never charged a fee to switch, and switching up or down doesn't reset your delivery day or preferences.

## Dietary preference vs. plan
Changing your dietary track (Standard, Gluten-Free, Vegetarian or Dairy-Free) is separate from changing your plan size. You can switch track without changing how many meals you get, and it's free - see the dietary options article.
