# Pricing, Plans & Billing

## Plan prices
FreshCrate offers three weekly meal plans, billed monthly (about four boxes a month):

- **2 meals/week** — $30/week ($120/month)
- **3 meals/week** — $42/week ($168/month)
- **4 meals/week** — $52/week ($208/month)

## How meals are priced
Every meal has a list price of **$17.50** — what you'd pay à la carte. The meals included in your plan each week are **free** as part of your subscription; you'll see the $17.50 list price struck through with "Free" next to it in your orders. **Add-ons** (desserts, sides, extras) are charged on top, whether the meal is a plan meal or an extra one.

Add-ons are priced by type: **sides $4.99, desserts $5.99, drinks $3.49**. The menu article lists what is on each dietary track.

Because your plan meals are free, a plan costs less than buying the same meals à la carte at $17.50 each:

- **2 meals/week** — $30/week vs $35 à la carte → you save **$5/week**
- **3 meals/week** — $42/week vs $52.50 à la carte → you save **$10.50/week**
- **4 meals/week** — $52/week vs $70 à la carte → you save **$18/week**

You can also order **extra meals** beyond your weekly plan. Extra meals are **not free** — they're charged at the $17.50 list price (plus any add-ons).

## Refunds
If a meal arrives damaged or is missing, you're refunded its **list price plus any add-ons** — this applies both to your free plan meals and to extra meals you paid for. A refund can be confirmed right in chat when it's **$20 or less** and you **haven't had another refund in the last 14 days**; anything larger, a second refund within that 14-day window, or a second refund on an order that was already refunded is reviewed by a human first. Fees — sign-up, pause hold, plan-change, and monthly plan charges — are not refundable. See the refunds article for details.

## Changing your plan
You can switch between the 2, 3, and 4 meals/week plans any time (while your subscription is active or paused). The change is **prorated**: you're charged or credited the difference in the weekly rate for the weeks remaining until your billing date, and your new plan starts the following week. There's no fee to switch. A cancelled subscription must be reactivated before (or while) changing plan.

## How pause billing works
Pausing keeps your plan reserved for a flat **$8/week pause fee**. When you pause, you're **credited** the value of each week you'll skip before your next billing date, minus that $8/week fee — so a 2 meals/week customer ($30/week) with 2 weeks until billing gets a **$44 credit** (2 × ($30 − $8)). After that, the **$8/week fee is billed on each billing date** for as long as you stay paused — that's $32 for a full month, or less for a part-month if your pause ends mid-cycle. This works the same whether you paused for a set number of weeks or indefinitely. When you resume, you're **charged** for the weeks left until billing at your plan's weekly rate, again minus $8/week. You can pause for 1–52 weeks, until a date within a year, or indefinitely.

## Cancelling and the reactivation sign-up fee
Cancelling is free and stops future boxes. Reactivating a **cancelled** subscription charges a one-time **$40 sign-up fee** — UNLESS you resubscribe *before your billing date on the same plan*, in which case reactivation is **free**. (Resuming a *paused* subscription is always free and immediate.)

## Your billing history
Your order history shows everything on your account in one place: each meal order (with what was charged), plus **monthly plan payments, sign-up fees, pause hold fees, plan-change prorations, and refunds**.
