# Pricing, Plans & Billing

## Plan prices
FreshCrate offers three weekly meal plans. You're billed monthly (about four boxes a month):

- **2 meals/week** — $30/week ($120/month)
- **3 meals/week** — $42/week ($168/month)
- **4 meals/week** — $52/week ($208/month)

Prices are per plan and don't change with your delivery day. Add-on items (desserts, sides) are charged on top of your plan when you add them to a box.

## Changing your plan
You can switch between the 2, 3, and 4 meals/week plans any time. The new monthly price applies from your next box — there's no fee to switch up or down. When you change plans in chat, you'll see a confirmation with the new monthly price before it takes effect.

## How pause billing works
Pausing keeps your plan and preferences reserved, so it isn't completely free: while paused you're billed a **hold fee of 20% of the value of the boxes you skip** (your weekly rate × the number of weeks paused). For example, a 2 meals/week customer ($30/week) who pauses for 2 weeks pays a $12 hold fee. You're not charged the full box price for skipped weeks — only the discounted hold fee.

## Cancelling and the reactivation sign-up fee
Cancelling is free and stops all future boxes. If you later want to come back, reactivating a **cancelled** subscription charges a one-time **$15 sign-up fee**. (Resuming a *paused* subscription is different — that's free and immediate.) You'll always be asked to confirm before the sign-up fee is charged.

## Refunds and credits
Refunds for damaged or undelivered orders go back to your original payment method. Small refunds can be confirmed in chat; larger refunds, or a second refund on the same order, are reviewed by a human. See the refunds article for details.
