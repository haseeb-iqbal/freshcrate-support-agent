# Referral Program

## How referrals work
Every active FreshCrate subscriber has a unique referral link under **Account → Refer a Friend**. When a new customer signs up through your link and receives their first box, you both get a credit applied automatically to your accounts.

## Referral rewards
You earn a $20 account credit for each friend who completes their first box, and your friend gets $20 off their first order. Credits are applied automatically to your next weekly charge and never expire while your account is active.

## Limits and eligibility
Referral credits are for new customers only — a friend who has had a FreshCrate account before isn't eligible. There's no limit to how many friends you can refer, but credits can't be redeemed for cash or transferred between accounts.

## Where to see your credits
Track pending and earned referral credits under **Account → Refer a Friend**. Earned credits also appear in your billing history and are applied before your card is charged each week.
